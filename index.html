Minecraft facts

Minecraft, created by Markus Persson and released by Mojang in 2011, is a hugely popular game that Microsoft acquired in 2014.
Featuring procedurally generated worlds and multiple game modes like Survival, Creative, Adventure, Spectator, and Hardcore,
it has sold over 200 million copies with a vast player base. Available on numerous platforms, Minecraft supports extensive community modding and multiplayer servers.
Its educational version aids in teaching various subjects. The game continues to evolve with regular updates, influenced by community feedback, and has inspired spin-offs
like Minecraft Dungeons and a range of merchandise.

Minecraft Dungeons

Minecraft Dungeons, developed by Mojang Studios and Double Eleven and released in May 2020, is an action-adventure dungeon crawler set in the Minecraft universe.
Unlike the main game, it focuses on combat and exploration, with procedurally generated dungeons full of enemies, traps, puzzles, and treasures.
Players can customize their characters with various weapons, armor, and artifacts, and the game supports solo and up to four-player cooperative play.
The story-driven campaign involves defeating the evil Arch-Illager, and the game is praised for its engaging mechanics, rich environments, and frequent content updates.

Minecraft: Education Edition

Minecraft: Education Edition is a special version of the popular game designed for classroom use, developed by Mojang Studios.
It provides educators with tools to teach various subjects, such as mathematics, history, and computer programming, through interactive and engaging gameplay.
Students can collaborate on projects, explore historical landmarks, and practice coding within a safe, structured environment.
This edition promotes creativity, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills, making learning both fun and effective.

Minecraft Legends

Minecraft Legends is an action-strategy game set in the beloved Minecraft universe, developed by Mojang Studios and Blackbird Interactive.
It introduces players to a rich narrative where they must lead allies in defending the Overworld from a new threat, blending real-time strategy and action elements.
Players gather resources, build structures, and command troops in dynamic battles against hostile forces.
Released to expand the Minecraft lore, Minecraft Legends offers a fresh perspective while retaining the franchise's iconic blocky aesthetic and creativity.
